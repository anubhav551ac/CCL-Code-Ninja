Soil Reference

Soil Types & Best Plants

.03
.01 Sandy

Sandy Soil

Drains quickly, warms fast in spring. Low nutrients — needs regular compost. Great for root crops.

Carrot · Potato · Radish · Watermelon · Peanuts · Cucumber · Garlic · Onion
.02 Clay

Clay Soil

Heavy, slow-draining but nutrient-rich. Needs aeration and compost to improve structure.

Broccoli · Cabbage · Cauliflower · Lettuce · Beans · Peas · Sunflower · Apple Tree
.03 Loamy

Loamy Soil

The ideal garden soil — balanced drainage, moisture and nutrients. Suits most crops.

Tomato · Corn · Wheat · Rice · Sugarcane · Spinach · Pepper · Eggplant · Pumpkin · Beans · Cucumber
.04 Silty

Silty Soil

Smooth texture, retains moisture well. Fertile and easy to cultivate when well-drained.

Tomato · Lettuce · Cabbage · Carrot · Onion · Peas · Potato
.05 Peaty

Peaty Soil

Acidic, high organic matter, moisture-retaining. Excellent for acid-loving plants.

Blueberry · Cranberry · Lettuce · Spinach · Carrot · Potato
